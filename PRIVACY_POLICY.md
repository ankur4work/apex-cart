# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 29, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Apex Cart ("App," "we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of merchants and customers who use our Shopify app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Merchant Information
When you install and use Apex Cart, we collect the following information:
- Shopify store URL and shop name
- Email address associated with your Shopify account
- Plan/subscription tier information
- App usage analytics (features used, clicks, engagement metrics)
- Theme and product data necessary to display the app block

### 2.2 Customer Information
Apex Cart collects minimal customer data:
- Anonymous click tracking on the Add to Cart button
- Aggregate analytics about button visibility and interactions
- No personal customer information is collected or stored

### 2.3 Technical Information
- IP addresses (for security and abuse prevention)
- Browser type and version
- Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)
- Timestamps of app usage
- Error logs and crash reports

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the Apex Cart app functionality
- Process subscriptions and billing
- Send service updates and security notifications
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Debug technical issues and troubleshoot errors
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 Third-Party Service Providers
We may share your information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating the app, including:
- Shopify (app platform and payment processing)
- MongoDB (data storage)
- Analytics providers
- Email service providers

### 4.2 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information when required by law or to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

### 4.3 Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

## 5. Data Retention

- Merchant account data is retained for the duration of your subscription
- Customer analytics data is retained for 12 months
- Deleted accounts have their data removed within 30 days
- Backups may be retained for up to 90 days after deletion

## 6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:
- SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
- Secure password hashing for account credentials
- Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
- Limited access to data on a need-to-know basis
- Compliance with Shopify's security standards

**Note:** No security system is impenetrable. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

### 7.1 Access and Portability
You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you and to receive a copy in a portable format.

### 7.2 Deletion
You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time. We will remove your information within 30 days.

### 7.3 Opt-Out
You can opt out of non-essential communications at any time. Essential service communications cannot be disabled.

### 7.4 GDPR and CCPA Compliance
For EU and California residents, we comply with GDPR and CCPA regulations, including:
- Right to access
- Right to rectification
- Right to deletion
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object

## 8. Cookies and Tracking

Apex Cart may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:
- Remember your app preferences
- Analyze app usage patterns
- Prevent fraud
- Improve user experience

You can control cookie settings through your browser.

## 9. Third-Party Links

Apex Cart may contain links to third-party websites and services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies before sharing your information.

## 10. Children's Privacy

Apex Cart is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

## 11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to countries outside your country of residence. These countries may have different privacy laws. By using Apex Cart, you consent to such transfers.

## 12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to:
- Know what personal information is collected, used, and shared
- Request deletion of personal information
- Opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information
- Non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

## 13. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

EU residents have the right to:
- Access their personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
- Restrict processing of their data
- Data portability
- Object to processing

**Data Processing Agreement:** We maintain a Data Processing Agreement with merchants in compliance with GDPR requirements.
